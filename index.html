Who We Are

A Win-Win, Collaborative Partnership of Professionals

RE/MAX Partners Real Estate has been assisting Southern California home Buyers and Sellers for over 30 years. Our message is simple — we are here to assist you and your family with all your real estate needs.

Our highly trained Sales Associates are able to assist in all fields of real estate including: Residential, First-time Buyers, Single and Multi-Family Properties, Commercial Properties, Investment Properties, Relocation, Inherited Properties, and Escrow.
