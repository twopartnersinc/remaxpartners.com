Our Story

A Legacy of Trust & Excellence

RE/MAX Partners Real Estate has been assisting Southern California home Buyers and Sellers for over 30 years. Our message is simple — we are here to assist you and your family with all your real estate needs.

RE/MAX Partners' longevity and success has been possible due to the professionalism and knowledge of every Sales Associate within our organization. Our highly trained Sales Associates are able to assist in all fields of real estate including:

  • Residential Properties
  • First-Time Buyers
  • Single & Multi-Family Properties
  • Commercial Properties
  • Investment Properties
  • Relocation & Inherited Properties
  • Escrow Services

All of us at RE/MAX Partners were honored to receive RE/MAX International's "Most Distinguished Office Award". Our Sales Associates are true professionals and work at being the best every day.

Specializing in Riverside and Orange Counties, with offices located in Corona & Tustin.
