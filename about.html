Our Story

A Legacy of Trust & Excellence

RE/MAX Partners Real Estate has been assisting Southern California home Buyers and Sellers for over 30 years. Our message is simple — we are here to assist you and your family with all your real estate needs.

Our longevity and success has been possible due to the professionalism and knowledge of every Sales Associate within our organization. Our highly trained agents are able to assist in all fields of real estate including:

  • Residential Properties
  • First-Time Buyers
  • Single & Multi-Family
  • Commercial Properties
  • Investment Properties
  • Relocation Services
  • Inherited Properties
  • Escrow & Closing

Specializing in Riverside and Orange Counties, with offices conveniently located in Corona & Tustin.
